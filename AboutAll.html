All of Us Are Dead is a South Korean zombie apocalypse series based on the webtoon "Now at Our School". The story follows a group of high school students trapped in their school after a zombie outbreak occurs. The series begins with a mysterious virus spreading through the school, turning the infected into bloodthirsty zombies. The remaining students must band together to survive and find a way to escape the school. They face numerous challenges, including the constant threat of zombies, dwindling supplies, and the breakdown of social order. The show explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the human condition in the face of extreme adversity. The characters must confront their own fears and weaknesses as they fight for their lives. The series is known for its intense action sequences, suspenseful storytelling, and emotional depth.
