Wednesday is a Netflix series that follows the titular character, Wednesday Addams, a gothic teenager with a penchant for dark humor and a fascination with death. She is sent to Nevermore Academy, a boarding school for outcasts and misfits, where she must navigate the social hierarchy, solve a series of mysterious murders, and confront her own family history. The show is a coming-of-age story that blends elements of horror, mystery, and comedy. It features a diverse cast of characters, including Wednesday's eccentric family members, her new friends at Nevermore, and a variety of supernatural creatures.
